Dynamic Coupling

Rigid-Bodies support physical-based two-way coupling with fluid (Gas & Liquid dynamics) simulations.
This means that the rigid-body affects the fluid flow and the fluid flow affects the rigid-body as well!

The default values are the physically correct interaction strengths but for artistic purposes you can change these here.

Dynamic Coupling::Solid-Fluid
Control how strongly the rigid-body (solid) motion affects the fluid flow.

Translational Strength





Define how strong rigid-body translations (Movements) affect the flow. 100% is the physically calculated value.

Rotational Strength





Define how strong rigid-body rotations affect the flow. 100% is the physically calculated value.

Dynamic Coupling::Fluid-Solid
Control how strongly the fluid flow affects the rigid-body (solid) motion.

Translational Strength





Define how strong flow translations (Movements) affect the rigid-body solid. 100% is the physically calculated value.

Rotational Strength





Define how strong flow rotations affect the rigid-body solid. 100% is the physically calculated value.
