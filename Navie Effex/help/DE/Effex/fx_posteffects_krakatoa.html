KrakatoaSR (Effex)

Effex now contains a direct bridge to the KrakatoaSR (NOT Krakatoa4C4D) renderer by Thinkbox Software.
This particle renderer (it can render particles as splatted points or as voxels) has mastered its way to a standard in the VFX industry to render particle data.

If you have a KrakatoaSR license you can now render Effex particles, Thinking Particles, C4D standard particles, splines and mesh points directly with the Effex Krakatoa bridge.
No annoying im- or export hassle. It's drag & drop and render. Especially for foam and spray renderings it is great.

If you have a Krakatoa4C4D license you can also render Effex particles. Simply use a POP::Cacher to save the particles into the Kraktaoa file format and then use Krakatoa4C4D to load that data).
