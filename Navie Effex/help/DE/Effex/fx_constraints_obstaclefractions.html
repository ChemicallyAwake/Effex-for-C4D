CS::Obstacle Fractions

Parameters
CS::Obstacle Fractions

Obstacles in a fluid simulation are volumes and therefore defined on a grid. If a volume's surface crosses a grid cell it may not cover the whole grid cell but only a fraction of it.

This fractional amount (between 0.0 and 1.0 while 1.0 means fully covers the grid cell) can be measured with this constraint.

You can use it to not let a node operate in grid cells that are taken by obstacles.
