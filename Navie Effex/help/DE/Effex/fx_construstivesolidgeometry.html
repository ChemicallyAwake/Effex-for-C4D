Constructive Solid Geometry (CSG)

The Constructive Solid Geometry node allows to perform boolean operations on Effex volumes. Same as the Boole object in Cinema 4D but on volumes instead of meshes. Allowing you to easily combine volumes, cut out areas of a volume or any Surface Reconstructor (for example to make the outside of a liquid super flat).

The CSG node is a volume itself, so it can be used in emitters, constraints etc.
