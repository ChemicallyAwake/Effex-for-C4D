ParticleFactoryTypes


Definitions of the four different particle subgroup types.

const int FACTORYTYPES


A global constant of the amount of particle types

ParticleFactoryTypes_Passive


Passive Particles. Usually used for ballistic setups.

ParticleFactoryTypes_Vorticity


Vorticity Particles. Usually used with the Particle Turbulence force.

ParticleFactoryTypes_Liquid


Liquid Particles. Usually used for...well, liquid simulations.

ParticleFactoryTypes_Diffuse


Diffuse Particles. Used for large scale secondary particles such as foam, spray and bubbles.
