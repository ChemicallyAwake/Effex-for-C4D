Drop here any forces (GF or DF) that should be applied to the rigid bodies.

Emission Mode





The type of forces emission.

1. Node - Each force & constraint is evaluated once at the node's center and then applied.
2. Mesh Points - Each force & constraint is evaluated for each mesh vertex.
3. Volume Cells - Each force & constraint is evaluated for each volume cell.
