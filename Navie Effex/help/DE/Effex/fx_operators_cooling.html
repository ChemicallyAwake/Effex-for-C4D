GOP::Cooling

Params
GOP::Cooling

The cooling operator has two purposes:

1. Cooling down temperature simulation values in a temperature channel.
2. Progressing fire simulation values of a fire channel.

Note: Of course you can apply this cooling node to any scalar channel you want.

Basically temperature cooling works like this: The higher the temperature, the higher the cooling rate
That's why flames can disappear so quickly (additionally to strong heat diffusion). High temperatures are cooled down almost instantly.
