DF::Vortex

Parameters
DF::Vortex

The Vortex dual force can be used as grid force (in the dynamics nodes) or as particle force (in a POP::Forces).

It generates a simple but predictable force forming a single vortex (rotating around a certain axis).

It's perfect to create single breaking waves in liquid scenarios but also for interesting rotation effects in any fluid dynamics.
