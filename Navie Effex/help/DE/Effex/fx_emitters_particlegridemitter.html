Emitter::Particle In Volume

Parameters Constraints
Emitter::Particle In Volume

The Particle In Volume is used to generate particles inside of a Volume and store them into a Particle Group of choice. It can generate any Effex particle type.

However, this particle emitter is completely based on a grid, so the global voxel size defines how many particles are generated in each cell.
Currently it generates 8 particles per cell. This is the particle density that is most useful for the algorithm used in the liquid simulations (FLIP) and at this time cannot be altered (also to guarantee a certain simulation quality).
