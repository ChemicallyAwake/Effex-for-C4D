GOP::Variable Density

Parameters
GOP::Variable Density

Calculates physically-based variable air density out of a temperature and smoke channel.

This means it is primarily used in a smoke or fire simulation because it requires a smoke and a temperature channel.

It outputs new densities into a custom scalar channel which can be linked in the fluid dynamics Variable Density slot.
