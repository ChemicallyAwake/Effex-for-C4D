Particle Geometry

Parameters
Particle Geometry

The Particle Geometry object allows to attach C4D objects (most of the time this will be geometry) to any Effex particle in order to render them.

All you need to do is assign a particle group and place objects to attach as child objects of the Particle Geometry (so in this case the hierachy matters).

Note: You can vary the input child objects to attach by using a GeoVariance node.
