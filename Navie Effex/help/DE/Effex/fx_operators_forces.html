POP::Forces

Parameters
POP::Forces

Use this operator to apply particle forces (PF or DF) to particles. This actually means it will change the particle's "Velocity" property.

However you can also add these forces to any vector property.
For example in a ballistic particle simulation you may want to fill the "Ballistic Force" property instead of the velocity itself.
