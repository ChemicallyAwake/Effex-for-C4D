DF::Follow Spline

Parameters
DF::Follow Spline

Creates a force that allows particles to follow a spline.

In contrast to the POP::Follow Spline all the forces are calculated on-the-fly and is used for more loosely coupled spline following (while the POP::Follow Spline will make particles procedurally follow the spline, here it is purely dynamic).
