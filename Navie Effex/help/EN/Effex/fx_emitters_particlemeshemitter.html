Emitter::Particle On Mesh

Parameters Constraints
Emitter::Particle On Mesh

The Particle On Mesh emitter is used to emit any Effex particle type into a user-defined Particle Group.

It will only generate particles on the surface of a polygon mesh. The advantage is that the particles can be filled with some extra properties containing surface information (normals, surface color, uv data..)
