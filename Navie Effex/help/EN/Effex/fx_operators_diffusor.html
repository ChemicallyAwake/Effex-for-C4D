GOP::Diffusor

Parameters
GOP::Diffusor

The GOP::Diffusor does what it promises: It diffuses (blurs) the data of a scalar or vector channel.

Temperature diffusion is a great purpose for it. Simply link the channel to diffuse.
For example this is mandatory in order to let fire spread over a fuel channel
