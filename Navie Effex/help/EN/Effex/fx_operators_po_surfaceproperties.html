POP::Surface Properties

Parameters
POP::Surface Properties

The Surface Properties node fills particles with properties that contain information about the current surface they form (liquid surface for example).
These information are required in a whole lot of places such as the POP::Foam/Spray/Bubbles or POP::Surface Tension node.

You get a better performance if you use a POP::Volume Properties node, so you can exclude particles based on particle density (and only catch particles near the surface).
