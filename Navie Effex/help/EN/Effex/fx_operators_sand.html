GOP::Sand

Parameters
GOP::Sand

This operator is specifically used for liquid simulations and turns a normal liquid into a sand simulation.
Simply add it to a liquid simulation scene, link the dynamics node and that's it!

The algorithm is grid-based which means it does not operate on particles but the velocity grid in the dynamics node.

Limitations:
This technique is very good for slow to mid-fast motions. It cannot handle fast motions very well (yet).
