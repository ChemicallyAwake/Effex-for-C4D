Liquid Dynamics [R]



Drop here the liquid dynamics to turn into sand dynamics.

Density Channel [O]



Drop here a scalar channel that contains variable densities. This is totally optional.
For example if you use the densities in the liquid dynamics node you can drop it here so the sand calculations can take them into account as well.

Friction



The amount of internal sand friction. You can set this higher than 1.0 but this is purely artistic.

Shear stress threshold



Sand piles up so the pile's inside areas become rigid while sand at the surface may flow loosely.
This depends on the shear stress the sand exhibits, which you can set the threshold for here.

Friction at obstacles



The amount of friction at obstacle surfaces.

Cohesion



This is a safety constant and adds a bit of loose motion to sand which helps with too rigid sand areas.
