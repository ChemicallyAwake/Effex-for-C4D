Parameters
POP::Deform

This operator allows any Cinema 4D deformer that can work on points alone (i.e. does not require polygons like displayer, bend, twist, ffd etc.) to deform any Effex particles! This gives you arbitrary options to art-direct your simulations.

Simply place it in an Effex scene containing Effex particles, link the particle group accordingly in the POP::Deform settings and then start adding C4D deformers below the POP::Deform node.
