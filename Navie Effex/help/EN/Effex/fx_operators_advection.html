GOP::Advection

Parameters
GOP::Advection

Allows to let a Grid channel or a Velocity field be dynamically transported by a velocity field (= advection).

You read correctly. A velocity field can be transported by itself (self-advection) and is used by the Gaseous Dynamics for example.

This is a purely grid-based (= eularian) approach very popular in CG/VFX to calculate dynamic transport of grid data.
