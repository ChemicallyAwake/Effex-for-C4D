DF::Field Curl

Parameters
DF::Field Curl

The field curl force will turn the result of all used constraints into a 3D curly force allowing any kind of procedural turbulence forces to be generated.

The power of this force is that it generates fluid-motion like vortex forces that are perceived very natural by the viewer.
Usually you start with a noise constraint to see the effect.
