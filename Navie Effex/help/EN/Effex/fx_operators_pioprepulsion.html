PIOP::Repulsion

Parameters
PIOP::Repulsion

When a particle collides with an obstacle it experiences repulsion forces. In layman's terms, it bounces of the surfaces.
Use this PIOP (in POP::Advection for example) to give the particles some bouncing and dragging behavior on surface collisions.
